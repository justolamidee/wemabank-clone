Our History

For 80 years, Wema Bank has been a force for progress, empowering people, businesses, and communities.

To the youth, we enable dreams. To the underprivileged, we bridge gaps. To the established, we drive success. To businesses, we fuel growth. To the finance industry, we set the pace. To the nation, we champion development. To the world, we promote innovation and sustainability. And to us, our customers are everything.

For eight decades, we have led the way. And with you, the journey continues.
